8. COOKIES AND TRACKING

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function and analyze usage.

  • Authentication and login
  • Security features
  • Load balancing
  • Session management

Analytics Cookies (Optional):

  • Google Analytics - Usage patterns and demographics
  • Conversion tracking - Ad performance measurement

Advertising Cookies (Optional):

  • Facebook Pixel - Retargeting and ad optimization
  • Google Ads - Campaign performance tracking

Your Cookie Choices

Browser Settings: Most browsers let you refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are sent. Note: Disabling essential cookies may prevent you from using our platform. When you first visit our website:

  • You will see a cookie consent banner
  • You can accept, reject, or customize optional cookies
  • Essential cookies are always enabled
  • Your preferences are stored and respected unless you change them

Opt-Out Tools:

  • Google Analytics: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Google Ads: https://adssettings.google.com
  • Facebook Ads: https://www.facebook.com/settings/?tab=ads

We also use similar technologies, including pixels, web beacons, SDKs, local storage, and tags/scripts. For simplicity, we refer to all of these as "cookies."

Our cookie practices are designed to comply with GDPR / UK GDPR / Swiss GDPR, CCPA / CPRA (California), U.S. State Privacy Laws, Australian Privacy Act 1988, New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, and PIPEDA (Canada).

When We Are the Data Processor:

For information about your customers (leads):

  • Contact details of people requesting quotes
  • Project information and service requests

You (the customer) decide how and why this data is processed. We process it according to your instructions.

Your Responsibilities as Data Controller

When using QuoteLeads for your business, you must:

  • Obtain proper consent from your customers
  • Provide your customers with a privacy notice

  • Process lead data only according to your instructions
  • Maintain security measures
  • Assist with data subject access requests
  • Notify you of any data breaches affecting your customers
  • Delete or return data upon your request

13. CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS (CCPA/CPRA)

Rights for California Residents

If you're a California resident, you have these rights:

  • Right to Know: What personal information we collect, sources we collect from, business purposes for collection, and third parties we share with
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (we don't sell, but you can opt out of targeted advertising)
  • Right to Limit: Limit use of sensitive personal information (we don't collect sensitive info)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We won't discriminate against you for exercising your rights

Do We "Sell" Personal Information?

NO. We do not sell personal information for money.

Targeted Advertising: We use cookies for advertising which California law considers "sharing." You can opt out via cookie settings or these tools:

  • Facebook Ads Settings: https://www.facebook.com/settings/?tab=ads
  • Google Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com

How to Exercise California Rights

Email: contact@quoteleads.com.au

Include:

  • "California Privacy Rights Request" in subject
  • Your name and email address
  • Specific right you're exercising
  • Details to verify your identity

Response Time: 45 days (may extend to 90 days with notice)

Verification: We'll verify your identity using information we have on file

Authorized Agents: May submit requests with written authorization
