FAQ

Common Questions

How does QuoteLeads generate leads? +

QuoteLeads uses a combination of ad campaigns, hyper-targeted audience data, and high-converting landing pages tailored to your trade. We drive real customers to exclusive enquiry forms or quote requests. Every lead is 100% yours, never shared with competitors.

How much does it cost? +

The cost of your QuoteLeads plan depends on several factors, including the package you choose and your preferred lead volume. Growth starts at $590 one-time setup. For precise pricing on Scale or Exchange, reach out to our team and we'll prepare a tailored quote based on your goals and budget.

Who does QuoteLeads work with? +

QuoteLeads systems are built specifically for specialised trades & home-services, the high-skill, project-based services that property owners actively seek out for larger jobs. We specialise in solar leads, roofing leads, HVAC leads, painting leads, carpentry leads, renovation leads, and landscaping leads.

Can I get leads sent to my existing CRM? +

Yes. QuoteLeads integrates with most existing CRM (Customer Relationship Management) systems. If you aren't yet using a CRM, QuoteLeads platform includes a streamlined lead management feature for tracking, nurturing and converting leads.

When will I start receiving leads? +

In most cases, you'll start receiving leads in less than 24 hours upon QuoteLeads launching your custom advertising campaign, or about 48 hours upon signing up as a new client.

How does QuoteLeads AI work? +

QuoteLeads AI Assistants are advanced artificially intelligent SMS workflows trained on your business. They instantly follow up with new leads to qualify them and book call-backs all without you lifting a finger. The AI assistants can also analyse patterns, score lead quality and suggest next actions. The AI speaks just like a real team member and follows your preferred sales process. You stay in control: turn features on or off anytime depending on how hands-on or automated you want to be.

Is there a lock-in contract? +

No, there's no lock-in contract, and no mandatory ongoing fee. You have complete flexibility to pause, adjust, or cancel your service at any time.

Do you provide customer support? +

Yes. Our dedicated support team is available 24/7 via Phone, Email & WhatsApp to assist with any questions or other concerns you may have.
