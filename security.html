Security & Compliance at QuoteLeads

Security, privacy, and responsible data handling are fundamental to how our platform is built and operated.

At QuoteLeads, we work with businesses that handle customer and lead information, and we design our systems with care to support secure, transparent, and responsible use of data.

This page describes the general principles, safeguards, and platform features we maintain to support secure operations and responsible messaging workflows. It also outlines how QuoteLeads approaches regulatory considerations, including frameworks such as the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA).

Our Approach

QuoteLeads is designed with the following principles in mind:

1 Security & Privacy First

Build and maintain systems with security and privacy as core considerations.

2 Responsible Communication

Offer platform features that enable consent-aware and responsible communication workflows.

3 Transparency

Encourage transparency, accountability, and appropriate data usage.

4 Continuous Improvement

Continuously review and improve safeguards as technology, risks, and regulations evolve.

Security Measures

QuoteLeads implements commercially reasonable, industry-standard security practices to help protect platform data, including:

Encryption

Data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL and protected at rest using industry-standard encryption methods.

Access Management

Access to internal systems is limited through authentication controls, role-based permissions, and additional safeguards where appropriate.

Infrastructure & Network Protection

Our platform operates within secure hosting environments and is protected by network security controls, regular patching, and system hardening practices.

Monitoring & Logging

We maintain system monitoring and logging mechanisms to help detect, investigate, and respond to unusual or unauthorized activity.

Ongoing Reviews

Internal reviews and assessments are conducted periodically to help maintain platform integrity and security posture.

Messaging, TCPA & Regulatory Considerations

QuoteLeads provides tools that may be configured to support consent-aware and responsible outreach practices, including SMS and email messaging. Platform features available to clients may include:

Consent-Oriented Workflows

Configuration options that support opt-in lead capture and permission-based messaging flows.

Opt-Out Support

Handling for common opt-out keywords (such as "STOP") and suppression mechanisms for contacts who have opted out.

Activity Visibility

Message and interaction records designed to support transparency and operational oversight.

Configuration Guidance

General best-practice resources intended to help clients make informed setup decisions.

Important Notice

Regulatory compliance—including compliance with TCPA, CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and similar laws—depends on many factors outside QuoteLeads' control. These may include lead source practices, consent language, message content, delivery timing, automation rules, and jurisdiction-specific requirements.

QuoteLeads provides a software platform and configurable tools but does not provide legal advice, make representations regarding compliance, or guarantee that use of the platform will meet any specific legal or regulatory obligation. Responsibility for compliance rests solely with the client using the platform.

Privacy & Data Handling

Data Minimisation

We collect and process information that is reasonably necessary to provide and operate our services.

Purpose Limitation

Data is used to deliver QuoteLeads platform functionality and related services. QuoteLeads does not sell personal data.

Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary for operational, contractual, or legal purposes and is handled securely throughout its lifecycle.

Individual Rights

Where applicable under relevant privacy laws, individuals may request access to or deletion of their personal information.

For more detail, please review our full Privacy Policy.

Incident Response

While no system can be guaranteed to be incident-free, QuoteLeads maintains processes intended to:

  • Identify and assess security incidents
  • Contain and mitigate potential impact
  • Notify affected clients where required by law or contract
  • Review and improve safeguards following an incident
